WARNING!

Hoarding of medicines such as; Phenylephrine hydrochloride, Chlorpheniramine Maleate, Paracetamol tablets, and Carbocisteine capsules are prohibited as stated in the Joint Memorandum Circular (JMC) No. 22-01.

People and establishments found to have violated the JMC will be held liable under the Price Act, Consumer Act of the Philippines, and other laws.
